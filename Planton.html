    About

    Planton is a 2 in 1 product as it works as both plant protectant & plant tonic. Planton is systemic in action. Planton protects crop from downy mildew, rots, blights & wilts caused by phytothora spp., soft rot & damping of caused by pythium spp., etc. It will be very much effective against pepper wilt disease and Arecanut fruit rot. Planton enriches the soil with potassium phosphate and thereby promote plants growth. Plantation dosage for both foliar & drenching is 4 to 5ml/ litre of water. Planton is best suitable for foliar spray & soil drenching.


    Planton could be one of the solutions for the crop issues such as downy mildew that can be seen in crucifers, grapes and vegetables, rots like Arecanut fruits rot, blights like in tomato & potato and wilts like pepper wilt disease. Application of Planton at right time would give a desired result.
