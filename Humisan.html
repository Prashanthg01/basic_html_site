    About

    Humisan improves root formation, biomass, stimulates plant enzymes, improves soil biota, and increases microbiological activity of soil, increases soil organic carbon and higher tiller rate in sugarcane & paddy. Humisan consists of humic acid 16%, fulvic acid 3%, solvents & other organic compounds 81%. Humisan is suitable for all crops. It can be used for foliar spray on the crop, seed treatment and seedling root dip. Humisan dosage is as follows: for seed treatment 5 -10 ml/ kg of seeds in sufficient quantity of water, for foliar spray mix 2-3 ml/ liter of water, for drip & sprinkler irrigation – 1litre/ acre and seedling/ root dip – 5 ml/ litre of water.


    Humisan with composition of humic acid 16%, fulvic acid 3%, solvents & other organic compounds 81%, would be one of the best products for organic agriculture. The core benefit of Humisan included adding organic carbon to soil, better root growth & more tillers in crops like sugar cane & paddy.
